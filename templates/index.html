Discount

As per Ministry of Railways, 0.5% discount in base fare of season tickets shall be granted in case of digital payment w.e.f. 01- Jan-2018.

Verification

Aadhaar verification for getting concessional tickets for Senior Citizens shall be on Voluntary basis from 1st January 2018
